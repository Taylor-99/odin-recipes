Meatballs

Description:

Satisfy your cravings for bold flavors with this Sweet BBQ Meatball recipe, a perfect blend of savory, smoky, and sweet. Juicy meatballs are coated in a rich barbecue sauce sweetened with hints of brown sugar and honey, creating a sticky glaze that's irresistibly delicious. The combination of tender meat and the tangy-sweet sauce makes these meatballs a hit at parties, family dinners, or even as a quick snack. Easy to prepare and packed with flavor, this recipe is a guaranteed crowd-pleaser.

Whether served as an appetizer on toothpicks or paired with rice or mashed potatoes for a hearty meal, these Sweet BBQ Meatballs offer incredible versatility. The sauce is made with simple pantry staples and can be adjusted to suit your taste, whether you prefer a touch of heat or a sweeter kick. Ready in just a few steps, these meatballs bring comfort food vibes with a gourmet twist, making them the ultimate dish for any occasion.

Ingredients:

Meatball Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1/4 cup grated onion
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

BBQ Sauce Ingredients:

  • 1 cup barbecue sauce
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1/4 teaspoon smoked paprika (optional, for extra flavor)

Steps:

Preparation Steps

  1. Preheat the Oven: Set your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Mix the Meatball Ingredients: In a large bowl, combine ground beef, breadcrumbs, egg, milk, grated onion, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Mix until evenly combined.
  3. Shape the Meatballs: Roll the mixture into 1-inch meatballs and place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  4. Bake the Meatballs: Bake in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until cooked through.

Prepare the BBQ Sauce

  1. Make the Sauce: In a saucepan over medium heat, combine barbecue sauce, honey, brown sugar, Worcestershire sauce, Dijon mustard, and smoked paprika. Stir well.
  2. Simmer: Bring the sauce to a simmer and cook for 5-7 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

Combine and Serve

  1. Coat the Meatballs: Transfer the baked meatballs to the saucepan with the BBQ sauce. Toss gently to coat each meatball evenly.
  2. Serve: Transfer to a serving dish and garnish with chopped parsley if desired. Enjoy as an appetizer or with rice for a meal!
