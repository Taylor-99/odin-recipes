Lasagna

Description:

Indulge in the comforting layers of this classic lasagna recipe, a timeless Italian dish loved by all. Rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, gooey mozzarella, and tender pasta sheets come together to create a symphony of flavors in every bite. Simmered with fragrant garlic, onion, and Italian herbs, the hearty sauce is the star of this dish, perfectly complemented by the creamy cheese layers. Whether you're cooking for a special occasion or a cozy family dinner, this recipe delivers a warm, satisfying meal that's as delightful to make as it is to eat.

Ideal for make-ahead meals or sharing with a crowd, this lasagna is both versatile and foolproof. You can customize it with your favorite ingredients, from ground beef or Italian sausage to spinach or zucchini for a veggie twist. Baked until bubbling and golden, each slice offers the perfect balance of textures and flavors. Serve it with a crisp green salad and garlic bread for a truly memorable dining experience that brings the taste of Italy to your table.

Ingredients:

Main Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 pound Italian sausage
  • 1 jar (24 ounces) marinara sauce
  • 1 can (14 ounces) crushed tomatoes

Cheese Layer

  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley

Seasonings

  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Steps:

Preparation Steps

  1. Preheat the Oven: Set your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the Noodles: Boil lasagna noodles in salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  3. Prepare the Meat Sauce: In a large skillet, cook ground beef and Italian sausage until browned. Add minced garlic, marinara sauce, crushed tomatoes, and seasonings. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Mix the Cheese Filling: In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, shredded mozzarella, grated Parmesan, an egg, and dried parsley. Mix until smooth.

Assembly Steps

  1. Layer the Ingredients: Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish. Add a layer of noodles, followed by the cheese mixture, and then meat sauce. Repeat until the dish is filled, finishing with a layer of meat sauce topped with shredded mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  2. Bake: Cover the dish with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 20 minutes, or until the cheese is golden and bubbling.
  3. Let It Rest: Allow the lasagna to rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing to ensure clean layers.
